Our Story

Darshak wasted an entire day and gas trying to purchase a perfect luggage bag. After hiking nearly a mile to find the right aisle, Darshak finally found the luggage store, but the model that he wanted was overpriced and sold out.

Exhausted from shopping, Darshak special ordered an overpriced bag, paid an enormous amount sales tax and a huge service charge to have it shipped to his house. He realized that there's got to be a better way!

Moreover,his passion for travelling drove him to start his own business. One more reason for Darshak's inspiration came from the fact that luggage bag size also affects the travelling experience of the travellers.
